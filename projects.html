The Bench Project

Location: Lafayette Community Garden

We are working in conjunction with the Mount Diablo Bee Keeper's Association to build benches at Lafayette Community Garden! This project aims to provide a place for gardeners, bee keepers, and visitors alike to relax and observe the bees at the garden.

Revival Garden

Location: ---

In this upcoming project, we will cultivate a garden dedicated to reviving and repopulating endangered plant species! Some local endangered plants we plan on saving are the Mount Diablo Buckwheat and Mount Diablo Fairy Lantern.
